# Career Shift Process
**Date:** June 2024
**Owners:** Global Head of HRPS, Global Head of Modern Development
[cite_start]**Purpose:** The Career Shift Policy aims to facilitate employees' transition to different roles within the organization[cite: 70, 72, 73, 77].

## Roles
* [cite_start]**Ciklumer:** Owns personal career development and often initiates the transition process[cite: 87].
* [cite_start]**Line Manager:** Current career supervisor responsible for professional development[cite: 88].
* [cite_start]**HR Partner:** Enables the process[cite: 92].
* [cite_start]**Mentor:** Assigned by CoE Leader to provide guidance and support[cite: 91].

## Step-by-Step Process
1. **Ciklumer Actions:**
   * [cite_start]Sends email to HR Partner about willingness for Career Shift[cite: 111].
   * [cite_start]Participates in skill development and self-learning[cite: 112].
   * [cite_start]Takes the HackerRank test for the desired role[cite: 115].
   
2. **HR Partner Actions:**
   * [cite_start]Requests Mentor assignment and gathers Line Manager feedback[cite: 119, 120].
   * [cite_start]Organizes the Technical Assessment (TA)[cite: 125].
   * [cite_start]Informs Ciklumer of results and next steps[cite: 129].

3. **Outcomes:**
   * [cite_start]**Recommended:** Demonstrated all necessary skills[cite: 146].
   * [cite_start]**Recommended (with conditions):** Substantial portion of skills met[cite: 147].
   * [cite_start]**Development Plan:** Partial proficiency; granted 3-6 months to prepare for re-assessment[cite: 149, 150].
   * [cite_start]**Not Recommended:** Did not demonstrate significant required skills[cite: 152].
