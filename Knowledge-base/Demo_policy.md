## 2. Workplace Flexibility
* [cite_start]**Hybrid Model:** typically 2 days office / 3 days remote[cite: 17].
* [cite_start]**Core Hours:** Teams generally overlap 11:00 AM – 3:00 PM local time[cite: 18].
* [cite_start]**Nomad Policy:** Work from a different country allowed for up to 30 days/year[cite: 19].

## 3. Code of Conduct & Ethics
* **Professionalism:** Zero-tolerance policy regarding harassment or bullying.
* **Conflict of Interest:** Side projects are permitted only if they do not compete with Ciklum.
* **Anti-Bribery:** We strictly adhere to the UK Bribery Act and FCPA. No employee may offer or accept gifts to influence business decisions.

## 4. Leave & Time Off
* [cite_start]**Annual Leave:** Generally 20–25 days (location dependent)[cite: 37].
* [cite_start]**Carry Over:** Up to 5 days can be carried to Q1 of next year[cite: 38].
* [cite_start]**Sick Leave:** Up to 5 days uncertified per year[cite: 40].
* [cite_start]**Recharge Days:** 2 extra "Mental Health" days provided annually[cite: 43].

## 5. Professional Growth
* [cite_start]**Certifications:** Reimbursement for AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, PMP, Scrum Master[cite: 46].
* [cite_start]**English Classes:** Free access for cross-border teams[cite: 47].
